CROWNSNEST RETREAT

We organise a retreat camp into a forests of Something Natural Area in Alberta, Canada. The camp allows you to withdraw for two weeks from all the stress caused by the world going on too fast, cities and billions of informations just rolling at us at all times. You'll find your inner peace and heal yourself from within.

Pure Nature

Living for two weeks in a tent in the middle of a forrest far from any distraction from the outside world.

Peace of mind

No electronic devices are allowed in the camp. No access to internet or mobile network.

Community

Cooking and eating together, workshops and activities in nature, sharing and bonding.
